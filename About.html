ABOUT US
The impersonal nature of the modern world has left some eager participants in the digital dust as the world passes them by. We’re here to slow the bus down and kick open the doors as we reach out across the country to pull on board all these growing destinations, rising travel agencies, hungry travel services and more. Travel Meet Travel is a cross country road trip under the guise of a traveling Travel Convention. Our team will be going across the country meeting members of the American travel industry to see who we can meet, help who needs help and get a better understanding of the travel industry, one unique person at a time. As we cross this vast country, we want to meet as many travel based businesses and the people who run them as possible. Whether you’re a member of a tourism board, a travel agency, tour operator, hotelier, tour guide, rental company, airline, theme park mogul, national park or anything else related to travel, we want to meet you, shake your hand and see how we can help. We’ve been in the travel industry for decades and yet with each meeting and every phone call we meet a person as unique as the last. Everyone has a new perspective and the best way to better understand the needs of the industry is to meet everyone, face to face. We understand the difficulties in finding the right partners to help your business succeed. Sometimes you need the technology, a payment gateway, a supplier to sell your inventory or another supplier to give you inventory. Some attractions need more attention, some destinations need a refresh or a hook to prosper like larger cities. Travel Meet Travel will put you face to face with everything you need as we make our way to your neighborhood coffee shop, your nearby mall or your favorite book store. We’ll be in your town with the contacts to get you what you need. We’ll be bringing along with us: payment gateway providers, GDS company, airline representatives, suppliers, financial representatives and more. If you would like to become a stop along our way or want to join the trip, please contact us below and we’ll be in touch.
