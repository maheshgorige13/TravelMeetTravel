SPONSORSHIPS
Travel Technology Experts The frontiersman who first conceptualized this intriguing country-wide journey is Ilyas Zameer, CEO and founder of TravelCreed, TechTuners, Brand2Fly, TravelsBond, Sporty Destinations and HYEBYE.com. Each of these companies address the different needs of the travel industry in their own unique ways. TechTuners has been providing travel technology platforms to travel agents, travel management companies, travel service providers, tour guides and loyalty programs for almost two decades. TechTuners builds, customizes and develops all sorts of travel technology. During the course of this trip Ilyas and TechTuners hope to find new ways to improve their existing technology to better serve the varied travel industry professionals we intend to meet. TechTuners would also love to find new concepts and ideas to build, test and develop for new clients across the American travel industry. Brand2Fly is a team of branding and marketing professionals. Brand2Fly began as part of TechTuners but has branched out to independently serve the travel industry providing a wealth of options for branding and marketing needs including corporate kits (logos, business cards, stationary) and trade show necessities (flyers, pamphlets, banners, posters, giveaways). If you need branding or marketing to breath some fresh air into your business or if you want to launch a brand new travel business, Brand2Fly is the go-to team. TravelsBond is a B2B marketplace which sources and collects inventory from hoteliers, activity providers, transfer services, car rental companies and cruises and resells them to a global network of loyalty companies, travel agents and travel managers. HYEBYE.com is a lifestyle portal where users are able to search for something to do at a destination or in their own neighborhood. HYE:BYE provides activities, airport lounge passes, transfers and special requests for important occasions.
