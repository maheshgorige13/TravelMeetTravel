TRAVEL MEET TRAVEL
Travel Meet Travel is an incredible and unique road trip designed around the travel industry to use travel to meet travel professionals across the country. Our journey will depart from San Jose, California to partake in a road trip. We will be setting up small and intimate events in select cities across the United States where we can meet tourism boards, travel agents, hoteliers, tour guides, activities providers and more. We want to meet you and see what you need, what you want and where you want to be. We are a bus full of travel professionals on our way to meet you. Want to know more, join in or sponsor part of the trip? Contact us, we would love to hear from you!
